About

I join the College of Intelligence and Computing at Tianjin University as an Assistant Professor. I received the Bachelor degree and the Master degree from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2011 and 2013. I received two Ph.D. degrees from Harbin Institute of Technology and The Hong Kong Polytechnic University supervised by Prof. David Zhang and Prof. Wangmeng Zuo in 2017 and 2018. My research interests include computer vision, deep learning and optimization methods.
