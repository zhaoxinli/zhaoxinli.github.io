About

I joint the VR lab at Institute of Computing as an Assistant Professor in 2016. I received Ph.D. degrees from Harbin Institute of Technology supervised by Prof. Kuanquan Wang and was a Postdoctoral Fellow in Department of Computing, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, supervised by Prof. Lei Zhang. I was also a visiting student at Department of Neurological Surgery of UMPC at University of Pittsburgh from 2011 to 2013, supervised by Prof. Mingui Sun. My research interests include multi-view stereo reconstruction, stereo vision, RGB-D, SLAM, 3D data processing and related 3D reconstruction areas.
